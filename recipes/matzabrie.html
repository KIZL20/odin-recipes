Matza Brie Recipe

Description

This Matza Brei is one of a kind.

Perfectly cooked and addictingly good.

Ingredients

  • Matza
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Soak Matza
  2. Break Matza
  3. Mix with egg
  4. add salt and pepper
  5. cook in pan with medium heat
  6. flip after light brown on bottom
  7. Cook for 2-3 more minutes
  8. cut and enjoy!
