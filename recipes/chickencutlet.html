Chicken Cutlet Recipe

Description

Perfectly crispy and juicy chicken cutlets.

Great for sandwiches and salads.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. set up three bowls, one with eggs, one with flour, and one with bread crumbs
  2. coat chicken in flour
  3. wash chiken in egg
  4. coat with breadrumbs
  5. heat oil in pan
  6. cook chicken cultets in pan
