Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a delicious multi-layered lasagna that takes like it's made from Grandma's kitchen.

The recipe is simple, the instructions are easy, and the result is delicious.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Cook Beef
  2. Cook Pasta Sheets
  3. layer sauce, pasta, meat, and cheese until dish is full
  4. bake for 45 minutes at 375 degress
  5. Let sit for 10 minutes when finished
  6. Enjoy!
